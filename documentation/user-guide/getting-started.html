Getting Started

Your first steps with SafeSphere

On this page

Welcome to SafeSphere

SafeSphere is a comprehensive security awareness training platform designed to protect your organization against phishing attacks through realistic simulations and interactive training.

New to SafeSphere? This guide will walk you through the essentials of using the platform.

Logging In

To access SafeSphere:

  1. Navigate to your SafeSphere instance URL (e.g., https://safesphere.yourcompany.com)
  2. Enter your email address and password
  3. Click Login
First Time Login? If you don't have an account, contact your administrator or use the registration link provided by your organization.

Understanding User Roles

SafeSphere has three user roles, each with different permissions:

Role Permissions Typical Users
Member View dashboard, complete training, see personal progress All employees
Trainer Create campaigns, view analytics, access training tools Security team, trainers
Admin Full system access, user management, configuration IT administrators, security managers

Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

For Members

  • Training Progress - Your completion status
  • Recent Campaigns - Phishing simulations you've received
  • Achievements - Badges and rewards earned
  • Leaderboard - Compare your progress with others

For Trainers & Admins

  • Campaign Statistics - Overview of active campaigns
  • Risk Metrics - Organization-wide security posture
  • User Analytics - Department and individual performance
  • Quick Actions - Create campaigns, manage users

Completing Training Modules

To complete a training module:

  1. Navigate to Training Modules in the sidebar
  2. Select a module from the available list
  3. Read through the content carefully
  4. Complete any quizzes or interactive elements
  5. Track your progress in the dashboard
Pro Tip Completing training modules unlocks achievements and improves your organization's security score!

Viewing Your Achievements

SafeSphere gamifies security awareness through achievements:

  • Training Badges - Earned by completing training modules
  • Awareness Awards - For identifying phishing attempts
  • Streak Rewards - Maintain consistent training completion
  • Special Badges - Milestone achievements

Access your achievements from the Achievements page in the navigation menu.
